Welcome to Handyman, Ghana's premier platform connecting you with skilled artisans for all your home and service needs. We're dedicated to easing the process of finding experienced professionals and providing a seamless way for artisans to connect with clients right here in the heart of Africa

Our Services

We understand the challenges of finding reliable help for various tasks. That's why Handyman offers a diverse range of services designed to offer accessibility and reliability for both clients and artisans. Our platform caters to various specialties, including:

  • Plumbers: Expert solutions for all your plumbing needs.
  • Painters: Transforming your spaces with vibrant colors and professional finishes.
  • Electricians: Ensuring safe and efficient electrical installations and repairs.
  • Masons: Building and repairing with quality craftsmanship.
  • Babysitters: Providing trusted childcare services.
  • And more!

We're committed to expanding our network to include even more skilled professionals, ensuring you find the right person for every job.

Our Mission

At Handyman, we strive to:

  • Ease of Access: Avoid the hustle of asking around. Our diverse pool of qualified professionals is just a few clicks away.
  • Flexibility: We respect your time and needs, offering flexible scheduling and a wide range of service options.
  • Reliability: We prioritize quality and trust, ensuring that every artisan on our platform is vetted and experienced.
  • Support Local artisans: We are dedicated to providing jobs for the skilled people of Ghana and Africa

Why Choose Handyman?

  • Local Expertise: We understand the unique needs of Kumasi residents.
  • Trusted Professionals: We carefully screen our artisans to ensure quality and reliability.
  • Convenient Platform: Our user-friendly website makes it easy to find and book services.
  • Community Focus: We're committed to building a strong community of skilled artisans and satisfied clients.

Whether you need a quick repair or a major renovation, Handyman is here to connect you with the right professional for the job. Experience the convenience and reliability of Handyman today!
